BoarderBucks (BDB) Whitepaper

Meme Coin Backed by Student Housing Rental Income

Abstract

BoarderBucks (BDB) is a community-driven meme coin that funds the creation and operation of a student boarding house. The project combines crypto culture with real estate investment, offering a token tied to a real-world income stream.

Problem

Students face limited affordable housing options. Meanwhile, many meme coins lack real-world backing and long-term sustainability.

Solution

By launching BDB, we raise funds to acquire and operate a student boarding house projected to earn $900 per month net. Rental income will fuel ongoing buybacks of BDB tokens, creating a sustainable ecosystem.

Tokenomics

  • Presale: 15% (150M BDB)
  • Liquidity: 20% (200M BDB)
  • Treasury: 25% (250M BDB)
  • Team/Advisors: 5% (50M BDB, vested)
  • Community & Marketing: 20% (200M BDB)
  • Buyback Reserve: 15% (150M BDB)

Use of Funds

Presale funds will cover property acquisition, renovation, furnishing, and initial operating expenses. A portion will seed DEX liquidity for smooth trading. Treasury funds are reserved for operations, maintenance, and potential expansion.

Buyback Mechanism

Once rental operations stabilize, 50% of net rental income will be used monthly to buy back BDB tokens from the open market. Purchased tokens will be burned or locked, supporting long-term token value.

Roadmap

  1. Token launch and presale
  2. Property acquisition and setup
  3. Rental operations commence
  4. Monthly buyback and reporting
  5. Potential expansion to additional properties

Risks & Disclaimers
